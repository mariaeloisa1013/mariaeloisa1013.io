THE PIXIES




FAIRY DUST! HELLO WE'RE THE PIXIES!

THE PIXIES is a kpop cover dance group based in Dubai, United Arab Emirates, performing since 2019. The group consists of 36
talented self-taught individuals who share the same passion for dancing, performing and love for K-pop. Since
debut, the group has been actively posting dance covers on their own Youtube channel with over 5.14K subscribers and
an accumulated total of 500K views, performing and representing kpop for many various events around the UAE.
